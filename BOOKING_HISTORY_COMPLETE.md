## 🎊 **User Journey**

### **📱 Accessing Booking History:**
1. **Login** to your account
2. **Navigate** to "My Bookings" in user menu
3. **View** beautiful dashboard with all bookings
4. **Filter** by status (All, Upcoming, Past, Cancelled)
